What is LUG@RU?

Linux User Group @ Ryerson University (LUG@RU) promotes the use of Linux, Unix, and FOSS as well as a place to come and ask questions about Linux in general. We also work on projects using Raspberry Pi's. Come to any of our meetings and have your questions answered!

Since 2017, the LUG@RU has come together several times each month to increase awareness about the many possibilities with open source, as well as hosting events on campus.

We welcome students in all faculties and programs to join us, regardless of programming experience.
